The Collector of Ancient Wisdom

Howard Schwartz stands as one of the most significant collectors and retellers of Jewish folklore in the modern era. A distinguished Professor Emeritus at the University of Missouri-St. Louis, Schwartz has dedicated his life to preserving and sharing the rich mythological and folkloric traditions of Judaism.

Like a modern-day Elijah collecting wisdom across generations, Schwartz has become the guardian of nearly a thousand unique tales that might otherwise have been lost to time. His work spans the ancient Middle East to medieval Europe, from North African Jewish communities to Eastern European shtetls.

"It is vital to pass on the love of the Jewish literary heritage to upcoming generations."
— Howard Schwartz

This mission has driven him to create more than thirty books, earning him prestigious awards and touching countless lives. From his home in St. Louis, the same city where he was born, Howard Schwartz continues to inspire new generations of storytellers and wisdom seekers.
